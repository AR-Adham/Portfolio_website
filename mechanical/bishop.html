Mechanical Innovation

This project is Bishop, an award winning, world championship playoff robot. This robot was ranked 18th in ontario and was a key part of an alliance that came 2nd in the hardest district event in the world. My role on this team was as the team captain, lead designer and the lead scouter. Primarily as a designer I worked on designing the Gearbox and chasis subsystems. I also assisted in the design of the intake and claw subsytems.
